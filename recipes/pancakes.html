Buttermilk Pancakes

Description

Pancakes can make or break any breakfast they're in. With this buttermilk pancake recipe you will never have to worry about your pancakes ruining your breakfast. These rich, fluffy pancakes will take no more than 15 minutes from start to finish but will completely change your breakfast game.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Buttermilk
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients into one bowl (flour, sugar, salt)
  2. Mix the wet ingredients into a separate bowl (buttermilk, milk, eggs and melted butter)
  3. Combine the dry and wet ingredients
  4. Make your pancakes!!
  5. Top with 100% maple syrup
