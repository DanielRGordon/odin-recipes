Picadillo

Description

Enjoy this recipe of my personal all time favorite meal, picadillo. It is a meal that is common in many hispanic countries all with their own slight variations. The one I am going to show is how my grandmother taught me how to make it, and I wouldn't change a thing.

Ingredients

  • Small chopped onion
  • 1/2 chopped green pepper
  • garlic clove to your taste
  • lean ground beef
  • 1 can tomato sauce (8oz)
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 2 sazon seasoning packages (I use Goya)
  • white sugar to taste
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. In an oiled skillet, cook onions, bell pepper, and garlic until softened. Stir often.
  2. Once those are cooked thoroughly, add ground beef into the pan. Cook until fully browned.
  3. Add in tomato sauce, raisins, sazon packets, sugar, and salt into pan. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Put lid on your pan and let the food simmer on low heat for 5 to 10 minutes.
  5. Serve with rice and enjoy!
